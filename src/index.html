the mission

In case you're reading this, my name is Sarina. I was born and raised in the foggy but bright city of San Francisco. Since childhood I seriously considered myself in particular an "ideas lady." Maybe I'll be an entrepreneur some day. Naturally, it starts with strong interests, but then the imagination takes over. I cannot promise it is becuase it is just not in my nature to sit still, but nevertheless having a vision does set the pace for finding a purpose.


Interests may include but are not limited to learning foreign languages, yoga, chess, tango, traveling, coding, boxing, BBQing, and playing the harmonica (future lessons). How is it that I having a vision does set the pace for finding a purpose.


I actually left America bound for Miami Ad School, Berlin (2019) specifically to train in art direction and copywriting. I was drawn to the ad world for the thrill of realizing ideas and see my imagination in action.
